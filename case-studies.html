2 of 5 Videos Drove $60K+ in Month One

2 out of 5 videos became winners in an industry where the owner told me 1 in 15 is the typical ratio — driving $60K+ in first-month revenue. Here's exactly how, and what the numbers say.

Meta Ads Video Creative DTC ROAS Active Campaign
Apr 2026

Bull Boulder Campaign · Meta

Spin the wheel. Pick a winner.

All five videos we shipped for this campaign. Spin to see them in roulette order, or pick one yourself.

The Creative Strategy

Each video was built around a different hook angle so the data — not a guess — could tell us what the market wanted.

  • Researched competitor creative on Meta Ad Library before writing a script
  • CTAs written for practical buyers, not generic drop-ship language

The Numbers, Last 30 Days

Flash Sale 1 | Video 4 — best performer across the board: 2.99× ROAS, $99.82 CPA, 1.68% CVR. Meta read it as a bottom-of-funnel closer, so the play is to protect spend and let the algo work.

Holy **** | Video 5 — volume driver: 945K impressions, 52.45% hook rate, $1.30 CPC. CVR at 0.84% points to a post-click gap (offer / LP), not a creative miss.

Hook Up Gone Wrong | Video 3 — turned off. 44% hook rate and a $0.63 CPC pulled clicks, but CVR sat at 0.25% and ROAS never crossed 1.02×. Wrong intent. Learnings folded into the next sprint.

The Takeaway

New industry, no playbook, a 2-in-5 hit rate against a 1-in-15 norm — and $60K+ in first-month revenue. Research before spend, hooks built for the actual buyer, and a testing structure that lets the best creative rise without burning budget on guesses. Still live and scaling.

From Zero to 59 Leads a Week, at $5 Each

A 17-week build for a local landscaper. Flat budget, creative refreshed weekly. Volume climbed, cost per lead fell, the inverse did the work.

Meta Ads Lead Gen Geo-Targeting Local Services
Mar 2026

The Setup

Local landscape company, no paid history. The brief: can Meta carry lead flow for a regional contractor the way it carries DTC brands?

The Approach

Flat $50/day. Creative refreshed weekly — winners scaled, losers paused. Every ad built around a single job-site moment.

  • TikTok-style cuts pulled from live install footage
  • Geo-targeted to realistic drive-time zip codes
  • On-platform lead form — no landing page friction
