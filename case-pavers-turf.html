Meta vs Google, Local Contractor Lane

Same buyer. Wildly different cost to reach them.

Google Search Ads
$8 to $30
per click
  • Auction packed with national franchises and lead-aggregator middlemen
  • High intent, brutal cost of entry for a local operator
  • You pay for keywords, not creative, no content leverage
Meta, Pavers and Turf
$0.76
per click, observed
  • Geo-exclusive, almost no local contractor running real creative in the same radius
  • Each install becomes the ad, content cost effectively rolls into the job
  • Lookalikes compound off real customers, not keywords

Observed CPC from this specific Meta campaign was $0.76. Google search CPC for the same vertical sits between $8 and $30. The point isn't the exact figure, it's the order of magnitude. One month of Google spend runs a full quarter on Meta.
