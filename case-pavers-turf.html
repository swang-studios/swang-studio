Changing How a Contractor Drives Business.

Pavers and Turf by O'Neill's had strong work, but no real system behind it. No paid social presence, inconsistent posting, and nothing set up to actually generate inbound leads.

This wasn't a quality issue — it was visibility. The installs were there, but nothing was packaging or pushing them in a way that brought in consistent business.

  • Built a content + ad system around real projects (photo, video, transformations).
  • Launched geo-targeted campaigns with clear offers and lead capture.

Everything shifted from random posting to a loop. Projects became content, content became ads, and ads brought in real inquiries. Simple, but done consistently.

Now it runs like a pipeline — not guesswork. From one-off installs to a content → ads → inbound leads loop that compounds.
