Content Design · YouTube

Clickbait Thumbnails for a Sports Creator.

Worked with a sports YouTuber (~70K subs) on thumbnails for big moments, NFL slates, Super Bowl content, live streams. The goal was simple: make something that pops instantly in the feed and actually gets clicked.

Everything was built around how people really scroll YouTube. Fast decisions, small screens, tons of competition. So instead of overthinking design, it was about making things obvious, bold, and easy to understand at a glance.

  • Simple 2 to 4 word hooks, strong reactions, bright colors that stand out.
  • Thumbnails + live banners turned around quickly for same-day relevance.

Over time, it became less about making each one perfect and more about building a style that works every time. Once viewers recognize the look, they're way more likely to click again.

Nothing fancy, just clear, scroll-stopping visuals that do their job.
